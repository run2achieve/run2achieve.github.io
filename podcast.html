What’s behind my podcast?

🌞
I am curious about other individuals, pondering whether they've danced to the same peculiar tune as me but remain hidden behind the curtain of unspoken thoughts and unshared escapades.
🌝
Many of my guests are introverts, so you can expect a one-of-a-kind atmosphere that stands apart from the extroverted dominance prevalent in social media, offering AI the choice to embrace their introverted side if they so desire :P
Season 1: "My Mental Journey to Migration"
Season 2: "Being ADHD" (planning)
S1E1: Monica - Boundaries, Growth, and Creating the Life You Want
Monica was born and raised in Taiwan. Monica pursued her PhD in the United States and embarked on a postdoc journey in the Netherlands. In this episode, we explore Monica's transformative experience of self-discovery and personal growth. Discover how she navigated the challenges of adapting to a new culture while maintaining her introverted nature and need for solitude. Gain valuable insights as she shares her perspectives on setting boundaries, finding balance, and actively shaping a fulfilling life abroad.
S1E2: Sarah's Journey - From Down Under to the Heart of Europe
Sarah, an adventurous Australian who has experienced life in Germany and now resides in the Netherlands. Join us as she candidly shares the challenges she encountered during her migration from Australia to Germany, offering a unique perspective on the cultural transition. Sarah will also provide an insightful comparison between her life in Germany and her current experiences in the Netherlands.
S1E3: Zephyr - Boundless Identity: Self-Discovery, Coming Out, and Sustainability Activism
In this episode, we have Zephyr. They grew up in London, spent years in the US, and now work in the Netherlands as an academic researcher. Zephyr is going to share their personal journey of self-discovery, explore their gender identity, discuss their experiences as a neurodivergent individual, and delve into their activism for sustainability as a scientist.
S1E4: James - From Rain to Radiance: Adjusting to My Disconnected Desert Life
Meet James, a British physicist who ventured to New Mexico, US, to build an astronomical instrument. His journey has been nothing short of a rollercoaster ride. Join us as he talks about how he adapts to new challenges - climate, food, transportation, accommodation, and many more!
S1E5: Felicia - Singaporean Finance to Dutch UX Design: Dare to Try, Dare to Thrive
Get ready for Felicia's incredible stories! During our chat, I constantly felt like I was at a fantastic stand-up comedy show (by the way, Felicia is considering doing stand-up).
A finance graduate, language wizard, and talented UX designer, Felicia resides in the Netherlands but originally hails from Singapore. Her journey is extraordinary – it all started with an awe-inspiring backpacking adventure in Europe that sparked her thirst for more. She next embarked on a thrilling 5-month expedition across Asia! Her passion for Japan led her to work there, immersing herself in its delicate and sometimes dodgy (you'll have to hear her story about her first day at work in Japan!) culture. However, her adventurous spirit eventually led her to the Netherlands for a 180-degree career shift into User Experience Design.
Felicia's ability to follow her heart and make her dreams come true is truly inspiring. Let's send her our best wishes as she continues to carve her unique path in life!
S1E6: Carmen - Invisible Struggles: A Dutch-born Chinese's Journey of Identity and Empowerment
Step into the world of Carmen, a Dutch-born Chinese whose life's intricacies unfurled long before she could fully grasp them. Amidst the cobblestone streets of her childhood and the corridors of her school, the weight of her ethnicity marked her, molding her responses and character in ways unseen.
She also spent a year and a half in the UK, gaining insights into the cultural differences between the UK and the Netherlands. Currently, she is visiting her relatives in Hong Kong. The recording of this episode took place remotely, with me being in Helsinki.
S1E7: Hiddo - From Netherlands to Nippon: My Life in Japan Through a Dutch Lens
Meet Hiddo, a Dutch astronomer who unexpectedly found himself living an adventure in Japan. Join him through his unique experiences: relishing the finest sake, enduring scorching summer mountain hikes (and surviving on streams in the mountains), challenging the cycling skills of the Japanese (very Dutch!!!), and discovering traces of Dutch heritage gracefully preserved in the charming city of Nagasaki.
I pointed out that as a person standing at a towering height (perhaps almost 2 meters!) with a fair complexion, Hiddo easily stands out in the crowds of Japan. However, he seemed to have no trouble fitting into the new culture. Want to hear more? Check out this episode!
